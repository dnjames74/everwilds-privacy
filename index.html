Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026

Everwilds is a wildlife island simulation game developed by Deon James. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Everwilds app.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account:

  • Email address — used to create and secure your account
  • Display name (username) — the name you choose to appear on the leaderboard
  • Game progress — your island state, coin balance, animals, and survival time
  • Leaderboard scores — survival time, peak population, and season reached
  • Playtime statistics — total time played and number of islands survived

If you choose to play as a guest, no personal information is collected and no data is stored on our servers.

🔐 How We Use Your Information

  • To save and restore your game progress across devices
  • To display your username on the global leaderboard
  • To track your playtime and islands survived in your profile
  • To send account verification and password reset emails

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

🗄️ Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. Supabase stores data on servers located in the United States and complies with industry-standard security practices.

You can read Supabase's privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

🍎 Sign In with Apple

Everwilds supports Sign In with Apple. If you choose this option, Apple may share a unique identifier and optionally your name and email address with us. We only use this information to create and identify your account. We do not receive your Apple ID password.

💳 In-App Purchases

Everwilds offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock the Tropical Biome. All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment or credit card information.

👶 Children's Privacy

Everwilds is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

🗑️ Deleting Your Data

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. We will delete your data within 30 days of receiving your request.

🍪 Cookies and Tracking

Everwilds does not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics. We do not track your behaviour across other apps or websites.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
